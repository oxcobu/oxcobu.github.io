Oxford Coding Buddies - OCB

OXCOBU is a community of school-aged students in Oxford enthusiastic about software development and engineering.

Primarily collaborating on Github, there are also in-person meetings and coaching sessions by experienced senior professional software engineers, supporting the scoping of projects, helping with technical issues, assisting with social and communication challenges and broadening intellectual horizons.

We do not tinker with canned projects merely requiring to follow step-by-step instructions, but do real stuff, with a sensible goal. It can get challenging. It can get frustrating. It can be rewarding - for those who're up to it.

Joining: By invitation.

Minimum requirements:

  • Regular free time
  • Reliable communication
  • Interest in building technical solutions to real-world problems
  • Ability to focus
  • Perseverance
  • Friendly attitude
  • A dedicated computer
  • Unrestricted internet access

You gain:

  • Software development experience
  • Team work experience
  • A project portfolio (the best reference of all)
  • Contact to mentors and future business partners
  • Like-minded friends

We love people:

  • With coding skills
  • With completed projects
  • With bold ideas and plans
